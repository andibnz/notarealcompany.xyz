How We Work

Our Approach

Every engagement follows our proprietary four-step methodology, which is largely the same as every other consulting methodology but has a different name and a nicer diagram.

01

Diagnose

We spend several weeks asking your employees questions they've already answered internally. This is called "independent analysis" and it costs significantly more than asking internally.

02

Design

We produce a large PowerPoint deck that says what needs to happen. It will have a 2×2 matrix. All consulting decks have a 2×2 matrix. This is non-negotiable.

03

Deliver

We help implement the things in the deck, some of which your team suggested in week one. We track milestones in a RAG status report where everything is amber.

04

Embed

We train your team to continue the work without us, then check in six months later to see if they need us again. They usually do. This is called "capability building."
